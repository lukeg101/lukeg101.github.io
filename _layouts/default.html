About me

I am a third year Computer Science Undergraduate at the University of Nottingham. I consider myself to be a 'hacker,' in both the casual and competitive senses with a keen interest in hardware, Object Oriented programming and Computer Science theory in general. I like to see 'how things work' and how I can better use them to solve a variety of problems. Often I find myself analysing elements of the world and trying to find a quicker, more efficient and better solution to a problem, whether there exists a problem or not. This mindset, I find, makes me a passionate software engineer.
